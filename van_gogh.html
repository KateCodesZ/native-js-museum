Vincent van Gogh

A Journey Through Emotion

Through February 9, 2025

The Vincent van Gogh exhibition invites visitors to explore the vibrant world of one of the most influential post-impressionist artists. Each piece reveals not only his artistic genius but also his passion for nature and search for harmony. This exhibition offers a rare chance to experience the power and beauty of Van Gogh's art, which continues to inspire generations.
