Welcome to the Museum of Fine Arts

With hundreds of works, our collections offer a journey through time and culture—from ancient artifacts to contemporary masterpieces. Recognized as one of Sweden’s leading art museums, we invite you to explore our diverse exhibitions and special installations, showcasing the richness of artistic expression. Join us for our free daily tours, where expert guides illuminate the stories behind the art.
